Redskin Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

You can't have Kielbasa and Sauerkraut without mashed potatoes. It's how my grandmothers always made it and that's just how it is.

  • 3lbs. Redskin Potatoes
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 11/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 Tsp. Duke's Mayo
  • 1 Tsp. Salt
  • 1 Tsp. Pepper

Equipment

  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Large Pot
  • Colander
  • Masher

Procedure

  1. Wash and quarter potatoes.
  2. Place potatoes and garlic in large pot and cover with water.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook until fork tender.
  4. Drain potatoes and garlic and return to pot.
  5. Add butter, milk, sour cream, mayo, salt, and pepper.
  6. Mash until desired consistency.
