Chicken Cacciotore

This is a recipe I found a long time ago when I first found out I was gluten free. Back at that time gluten free pastas were not so great and as a result I've always made it with rice on the side.

Ingredients

  • 4lbs. Chicken Thighs and Drums
  • 1 Medium Yellow Onion Sliced
  • 2 Bell Pepper Sliced
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata Olives
  • 1 large can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 3 tsp Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 cup Red Wine
  • 1/2 cup Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Parsley

Equipment

  • Skillet
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Large Pot

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Place chicken skin side down in skillet and brown.
  4. Remove chicken and set aside.
  5. Add onion and bell pepper to skillet and sautee.
  6. Add garlic and olives to skillet and continue sauteeing.
  7. Add tomato paste and cook until fragrant.
  8. Add red wine and cook until reduced by half.
  9. Add chicken stock and crushed tomatoes.
  10. Place chicken back in skillet skin side up.
  11. Cover and place in oven for 1 hour.
  12. While chicken is cooking, cook rice according to package instructions.
  13. Once chicken is done, remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  14. Serve and garnish with parsley.
